Modeling Invasive Species Dynamics and Distribution

Welcome! This research contains the research and code used in the study "Modeling Invasive Species Dynamics and Distribution to Guide Management Strategies: A Case Study on Cane Toads in Australia" by Hoang Le. The research aims to comprehensively assess potential cane toad distribution in Australia using statistical and machine learning models.

What is it?

Welcome to my research study! This is a study on SDM - Species Distribution Modeling on a species - In this case a cane toad for proof of concept. The study employs several modeling approaches to predict the presence and spread of Cane Toads in Australia, including Generalized Linear Models (GLM), BIOCLIM, Domain, Support Vector Machine (SVM), MaxEnt, and Random Forest. The repository includes the final paper that provides a detailed account of the methodology, the data analyzed, model evaluations, and the results, including a geospatial prediction map. The study only employed geospatial data, and not taking predatory into consideration yet.

Study Purpose and Objective

Pure fun of learning! I want to explore how a species of animal may have different distribution model based on different/ similar environmental factor. This research will expand out into taking predatory into consideration, and taking different animals into consideration as well.

Project Contents

Data Files:

  • data.zip – Species occurrence data (large raster layers >100MB).
  • input.zip – Input datasets for modeling species occurrence.
  • Terrestrial Ecoregions – Refer to external package documentation for downloading full data.

R Markdown Scripts:

  • AbsenceBGData.Rmd – Analyzes absence and background data of invasive species.
  • DataPipeline_ctsel.Rmd – Builds a data pipeline for Cane Toads under the ctsel dataset.
  • DifferentModelMethods.Rmd – Explores ML algorithms for modeling Cane Toad spread.
  • ModelFitting.Rmd – Fits predictive models for species presence and spread.

Outputs & Documents:

  • Figures/ – Visual outputs from modeling and analysis.
  • DA401 - Autobiography - Hoang Le.pdf – Autobiographical context of the study.
  • README.md – Repository description and usage guide.
  • LICENSE.txt – BSD-3-Clause open source license.

Project Configuration:

  • .Rproj – R project file for environment setup.
  • .Rhistory – Script and command history.
  • .gitignore – Version control exclusions.
